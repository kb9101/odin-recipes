Strawberry Cake

Description

I made this as a breakfast cake but it is so good that my family eats it for dessert. I baked mine in a cast iron skillet but a springform pan will also work.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • ½ cup buttermilk, at room temperature
  • 1 (16 ounce) package strawberries, diced
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 10-inch cast iron skillet.
  2. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. Cream butter and 1 cup sugar with an electric mixer together in another bowl until light and fluffy. Add eggs, 1 at a time, mixing well after each addition. Add vanilla.
  4. Add dry ingredients and buttermilk alternately. Fold in strawberries.
  5. Transfer batter into the prepared skillet and smooth out the top. Sprinkle with 2 tablespoons sugar.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, 60 to 75 minutes.
